01

Hot Top Casting

The journey begins with premium quality aluminium billets sourced for consistency and purity. Our state-of-the-art Hot Top Casting facility ensures homogeneous alloy composition, free from impurities, setting the foundation for high-strength extrusions. Every batch undergoes rigorous inspection to guarantee top-grade raw material quality.

Automated Zero-Defect Pure Alloy ISO Certified
02

Precision Extrusion

Equipped with high-tonnage PLC-controlled presses (up to 4000 MT), we extrude complex aluminium profiles with microscopic precision. Our automated systems ensure uniform grain structure, dimensional accuracy, and superior surface finish for every profile. This process allows for versatile shapes suitable for architecture, industrial, and renewable energy applications.

4000 MT Press PLC Control High Precision
03

Surface Finishing

From Anodizing to Powder Coating, our finishing lines provide exceptional durability and premium aesthetics. Using eco-friendly chemicals and advanced automation, we ensure a finish that resists corrosion, scratches, and fading for decades. Each profile is carefully inspected to maintain consistent color, texture, and performance.

Commitment to Net Zero

Zero Discharge

Advanced effluent treatment plants ensure zero liquid discharge, protecting soil and water resources. Every process is designed to minimize environmental footprint and ensure regulatory compliance.

Solar Powered

Approximately 30% of our energy needs are met through captive solar power plants. Our commitment to renewable energy reduces carbon emissions and promotes sustainable manufacturing practices.

Infinite Recycling

Aluminium is the green metal. Our advanced recycling unit processes scrap efficiently, minimizing the need for mining while ensuring high-quality reusable aluminium, contributing to a circular economy.
