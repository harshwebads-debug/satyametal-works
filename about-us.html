Milestones

Our Journey

2012

The Inception

Founded with a vision to revolutionize aluminium extrusion. Established our first plant in Bhiwadi, Rajasthan.

2015

Expansion Phase

Commissioned two new automated extrusion presses to meet growing demand. Production capacity doubled.

2018

Global Standards

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Started exporting high-precision profiles to international markets.
